Short Bio

Lu, Hong (Lewis) recieved B.Eng. in Computer Engineering from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China in 2019, supervised by Dr. Bryce Zhai. He was admitted to Cornell University, New York, United States, majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Due to CoronaVirus breakout, he is now with IUSL at Westlake Institute for Advanced Study, Hangzhou, China, lead by Principal Investigator Dr. Shiyu Zhao.

Currently, he is pursuing MSc Robotics at The University of Sheffield, UK starting from Oct.2020.

His main research interests including Path Planning, Obstacle Avoidance for robots, Deep Learning application in multi-robot system.

He likes playing basketball since childhood.
